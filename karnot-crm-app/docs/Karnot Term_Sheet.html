VI. Risk Factors & Mitigation

Technology & Market Risk

Risk: PFAS ban timeline delays or technology adoption slower than projected.

Mitigation: Regulations already enacted (EU 2027, USA states 2029-2032). Technology proven with 10,000+ European deployments. Natural refrigerants are permanent solution regardless of timeline.

Competitive Risk

Risk: Large HVAC manufacturers enter natural refrigerant market.

Mitigation: 2-3 year first-mover advantage. BOI-SIPP registration creates regulatory moat. Strategic partner network (36 energy managers) provides distribution advantage. Proprietary FLX™ Defrost technology differentiates product.

Execution Risk

Risk: Scaling challenges or key person dependencies.

Mitigation: Conservative projections assume measured growth. CFO hire Q1 2026 strengthens governance. Board includes independent director. Founder has proven exit (Likido/Dalrada).

Currency Risk

Risk: Philippine peso volatility affects USD-denominated costs.

Mitigation: 70%+ revenue is export/USD-denominated. BOI duty exemptions reduce import exposure. Natural hedge through pricing strategy.

Capital Risk

Risk: Additional capital needed beyond seed round.

Mitigation: Profitable Year 1 (2026). Positive operating cash flow Q4 2026. Series A planned 2027 ($1.5M) for growth acceleration, not survival. Strong unit economics (23:1 LTV:CAC) support organic growth.
