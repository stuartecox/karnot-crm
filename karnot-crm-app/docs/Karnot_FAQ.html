TECHNOLOGY & PRODUCT
What makes the tech different?
We exclusively use Natural Refrigerants (CO₂ & R290) which are future-proof against PFAS/F-Gas bans. Our proprietary iSAVE AI software optimizes performance in real-time, delivering 40-60% energy savings.

Key Differentiators:
  • Oak Ridge validation: 30-35% higher COP than incumbent systems
  • Zero PFAS: Compliant with EU/ECHA proposed bans (competitors face stranded assets)
  • Simultaneous output: 121°C hot water + -30°C cooling from single unit
  • FLX Defrost: 50% energy reduction in cold climates, zero capacity loss
  • Blockchain carbon credits: Verified emission reductions tradeable on voluntary markets
Are the systems safe?
Yes. We use a Monoblock Architecture, which keeps the refrigerant charge (R290/Propane) 100% outdoors. No flammable gas ever enters the building. Compliant with EN378 safety standards.

Safety Features:
  • All refrigerant contained in outdoor unit
  • Leak detection with automatic shutoff
  • Fire-rated enclosure for R290 units
  • CE, UL, TÜV certified
  • Insurance-approved for commercial installations
What is the product warranty and expected lifespan?
  • Compressor: 5-year manufacturer warranty (Panasonic/Copeland)
