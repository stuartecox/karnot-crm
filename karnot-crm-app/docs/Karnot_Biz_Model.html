5.3 Revenue Stream 2: Energy-as-a-Service (EaaS) — Dual-Channel

EaaS is our primary recurring revenue engine. We operate two parallel delivery channels under the same iSAVE edge platform: a utility-led residential model (AQUAHERO) and a direct commercial model (iHEAT / iCOOL). Both share the same hardware stack, edge controller, and VPP backbone — expanding our total addressable market across every segment simultaneously.

5.5 Distribution: The "Partner-Equity" Network

To scale globally without bloating fixed costs, we deploy two distinct partner strategies adapted to our core markets:

🇵🇭

Domestic Market
(The "Co-Owner" Model)

We recruit elite energy professionals (ESCOs, Engineers) as Partner-Investors. They invest $2.5k – $5k to buy "skin in the game," receiving quarterly dividends and a permanent hardware discount. This turns "mercenary" dealers into loyal co-owners.

🇬🇧

UK Export Market
(The "Service" Model)

We view the UK's 5,000+ MCS Certified Installers as our primary sales force. We win them by solving their #1 bottleneck: Admin. Our Philippines-based "Concierge Team" handles their complete grant paperwork and system design for free. We don't just sell them a heat pump; we sell them time.

5.6 The iSAVE VPP Smart Controller: Grid Intelligence Engine

The iSAVE Controller acts as the "Central Brain" of our distributed energy network. Unlike passive monitoring tools, this is an Active Control System built on a robust ARM Cortex-class MCU designed to protect revenue, extend asset life, and unlock grid-level value.

Key Capabilities:
  • Real-Time Performance Monitoring: Tracks COP, energy consumption, and system health 24/7
  • Predictive Maintenance AI: Machine learning algorithms detect anomalies weeks before failure
  • Grid Integration: Enables demand response and Virtual Power Plant (VPP) functionality
  • Blockchain MRV: Creates verifiable, tradeable carbon credits from measured energy savings
  • Remote Diagnostics: Reduces service calls and enables our Call Centre to troubleshoot remotely

6. Locations & Facilities

6.1 The Cosmos Farm Innovation Centre

Karnot Energy Solutions Inc. is headquartered at Cosmos Farm in Mapandan, Pangasinan. This strategic location serves as our corporate command centre, primary technology demonstration site, and a fully integrated "Living Laboratory." Our vision is to create a solar-powered microgrid ecosystem that seamlessly combines commercial operations with real-world applications of our technology. The facility proves that our "Twin-Engine" strategy (Tropical Efficiency + Arctic Resilience) works in practice.

The development of the facility is structured in three distinct phases to align operational capacity with capital deployment.

Phase 1

  • North American Approvals (Q3): Execution of Mexican NOM and Canadian CSA certification. Field testing of CO₂ Monoblock in Vancouver.

6.3 Location Advantage

Located in Central Luzon, our OPEX base is significantly lower than Metro Manila. Direct access to the TPLEX (Tarlac-Pangasinan-La Union Expressway) ensures rapid logistics for both domestic distribution and transport to international shipping ports.

6.4 Territorial Distribution Strategy

To drive rapid adoption across Northern Luzon without cannibalising sales, Karnot is implementing a "One Partner, One Zone" exclusivity policy. We have identified 17 Strategic Zones across the region. In 2026, our primary objective is to recruit one specialist engineering firm or ESCO in each zone to act as the "Authorised Territory Distributor."

20km Exclusivity: Each partner is granted a protected 20km radius around their base of operations. All leads generated by Karnot's central marketing within that zone are handed exclusively to them, with realistic sales 'minimal' performance targets to meet.
