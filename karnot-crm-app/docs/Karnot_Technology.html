❄️ Natural Refrigerant Portfolio

Unlike competitors relying on synthetic HFCs and HFOs (PFAS chemicals), Karnot exclusively uses natural refrigerants with zero environmental impact. Each substance is optimized for specific temperature ranges and applications.

-38°C to +140°C

Carbon Dioxide (CO₂ - R744)

Industrial High-Temperature Platform

  • GWP: 1 (industry baseline)
  • ODP: 0 (non-ozone depleting)
  • Applications: Process heat, steam generation, hot water
  • Pressure: High-pressure transcritical cycle
  • Efficiency: Superior volumetric capacity
  • Safety: Non-toxic, non-flammable
+15°C to +75°C

Propane (R290)

Commercial & Residential Systems

  • GWP: 3 (99.8% lower than R410a)
  • ODP: 0
  • Applications: Space heating, domestic hot water, light commercial
  • Efficiency: A+++ energy rating achievable
  • Charge: Minimal refrigerant quantity required
  • Safety: A3 classification (flammable but manageable)
+90°C to +200°C

Water (H₂O - R718)

Steam & High-Temperature Heat Recovery

  • GWP: 0
  • ODP: 0
  • Applications: Mechanical Vapor Recompression (MVR), steam generation
  • Technology: Turbo-compressor with energy recovery
  • Efficiency: Up to 75% waste heat recovery
  • Cost: Virtually free refrigerant
-38°C to -160°C

Air (R729)

Cryogenic & Ultra-Low Temperature

  • GWP: 0
  • ODP: 0
  • Applications: Biomedical storage, food preservation, gas liquefaction
  • Technology: Air cycle with turbo-expander
  • Benefit: No refrigerant refills required
  • Safety: Completely inert and safe

🚫 Why We Don't Use Synthetic Refrigerants

The majority of HFCs and new-generation refrigerants belong to the PFAS family—"forever chemicals" linked to serious health risks and environmental persistence. Karnot categorically refuses to use these substances.

Health Impacts of PFAS

  • • Developmental issues (low birth weight)
  • • Increased cancer risk (kidney, testicular)
  • • Immune system suppression
  • • Liver damage and thyroid disease
  • • Cholesterol level alterations
  • • Reproductive issues

Regulatory Crackdown

  • • EU ECHA: Proposed ban on 10,000+ PFAS substances
  • • Kigali Amendment: 80% HFC phase-down by 2036
  • • US EPA: PFAS Strategic Roadmap enforcement
  • • Result: R410a, R32, R454B systems become stranded assets
