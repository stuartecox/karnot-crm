01 — The Market Gap

Millions of Heat Pumps. None of Them Handle Defrost Well.

The hidden energy penalty: Every air-source heat pump in a cold or humid climate runs defrost cycles. Conventional approaches — reverse-cycle defrost or electric resistance heaters — impose a 5–10% seasonal COP penalty. For a 35 kW unit running year-round, this is 9.25 kWh wasted per defrost cycle, 4–6 times per day. FLX eliminates this penalty at source, using heat the compressor was already generating and discarding through the gas cooler.

02 — The Retrofit

A Single Service Visit. Permanent Improvement.

Retrofit requirement: The host system must use CO₂ (R744) as the working refrigerant — transcritical or cascade. FLX taps the high-pressure liquid line via a three-port manifold. The FLX tank (190mm × 500mm) mounts alongside the gas cooler unit. Total install time: approximately 4 hours with a CO₂-certified refrigeration engineer.

1

Site Survey & System Assessment

Confirm CO₂ refrigerant, map high-pressure liquid line routing, assess mounting location. Document baseline defrost cycle frequency and energy draw. ~30 min.

2

Three-Coil Manifold Installation

System pumped down. Three-port manifold installed: (1) subcooling coil feed to FLX tank, (2) existing gas cooler return, (3) defrost coil discharge to evaporator. Re-pressurised, leak-tested to 150 bar. ~2 hrs.

3

FLX Tank Fill — FLX Fluid 2.0

Tank filled with FLX Fluid 2.0 (coconut oil + graphene + beeswax + PEG PCM blend) from sealed 4L service cans. Copper foam matrix pre-installed. Ultrasonic transducers wired to iSAVE controller. ~45 min.

4

iSAVE Controller Commissioning

iSAVE edge controller installed (DIN-rail, RS485 ModBus). Fan motor differential pressure sensor wired for defrost trigger. System test: one full charge-discharge cycle confirmed. LTE-M registration to Karnot Control Centre. ~45 min.

5

Performance Baseline & Sign-Off

First 24 hrs logged: charge rate (target 4.5 kWh/hr), defrost cycle duration, evaporator temp stability, electric heater draw (target: zero). Customer sign-off report generated. FLX Fluid 2.0 next service date registered. ~30 min.
