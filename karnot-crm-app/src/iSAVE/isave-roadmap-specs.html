Heat Pump Management

Full monitoring and control of Karnot iHEAT heat pumps via ModBus RS485. Smart scheduling, defrost management, and demand response.

  • Compressor on/off & speed control
  • Defrost cycle detection & optimisation
  • Inlet/outlet temperature monitoring
  • COP calculation in real time
  • Refrigerant pressure trend monitoring
  • DR curtailment: on/off + load reduction
  • Pre-conditioning before DR events
☀️

Solar PV Integration

Read generation data from inverters (SunSpec ModBus / Enphase / SolarEdge API). Maximise self-consumption and store surplus in thermal or electric storage.

  • Real-time generation vs. consumption
  • Auto-dispatch to thermal battery on surplus
  • Auto-charge electric battery on surplus
  • Grid export limiting where required
  • Time-of-use tariff awareness
  • Solar forecast integration (weather API)
🏦

Thermal Battery Control

Charge/discharge Karnot thermal storage tanks. Use low-cost or solar energy to store heat or cold, dispatch during peak tariff or DR events.

  • State of Charge (SoC) monitoring
  • Temperature stratification sensing
  • Charge during off-peak / solar surplus
  • Discharge during peak or DR event
  • Round-trip efficiency tracking
  • Insulation loss modelling
🔋

Electric Battery (BESS)

Interface with third-party BESS via CAN bus or ModBus. Coordinate charge/discharge with solar, tariff schedule, and DR signals.

  • Battery SoC, SoH monitoring
  • Charge from solar / off-peak grid
  • Discharge to offset peak demand
  • DR event: battery discharge to reduce load
  • Cycle counting and degradation tracking
  • Compatible: BYD, Pylontech, CATL

Demand Response Switching

Automated load switching in response to utility DR signals (OpenADR 2.0), price spikes, or frequency deviation. Precision control of which assets to curtail and for how long.

  • Minimum comfort temperature limits
  • Asset priority ranking for curtailment
  • Rebound management (staggered recovery)
  • DR event logging for utility verification

Preventive Maintenance AI

On-device TinyML models analyse Utterberry sensor streams to detect anomalies before failures occur. Automated service alerts to Karnot engineers.

  • Compressor vibration anomaly detection

Utterberry Partnership

Utterberry Smart Sensor Network

Ultra-low-power wireless sensor motes for real-time heat pump health monitoring — developed by Dr. Heba Bevan

🌡️

Temperature Sensors

Multi-point temperature mapping across heat pump refrigerant circuit, water circuit, and ambient environment. ±0.1°C accuracy.

  • Refrigerant suction & discharge temp
  • Water inlet & outlet temperatures
  • Ambient air temperature
  • Thermal battery zone temperatures
  • Evaporator coil surface temp (ice detection)
💧

Humidity Sensors

Indoor and outdoor humidity monitoring for optimal heat pump operation and defrost cycle management. Critical in Philippine tropical climate.

  • Indoor zone relative humidity
  • Outdoor ambient humidity
  • Evaporator coil humidity (frost risk)
  • Condensate management alerts
  • Mould risk scoring per zone
📳

Vibration Sensors

Tri-axis accelerometer motes mounted on compressor housing and pipework. Fast Fourier Transform (FFT) analysis for bearing and mechanical fault detection.

  • Compressor bearing wear signatures
  • Fan motor imbalance detection
  • Pipe vibration (resonance alerts)
  • On/off cycle detection & count
  • Vibration trend over time (degradation)
❄️

Defrost & Ice Detection

Surface temperature + humidity fusion to predict and detect ice build-up on the evaporator. Optimise defrost cycles — avoid unnecessary defrost in Philippines tropical climate.

  • Ice formation prediction model
  • Defrost cycle trigger & duration optimisation
  • Defrost completion confirmation
  • COP recovery tracking post-defrost
📡

Wireless Mesh Network

Utterberry motes form a self-healing wireless mesh — no cabling required. Motes relay data hop-to-hop to the iSAVE gateway, which uploads to cloud.

  • Sub-GHz ISM band (868/915 MHz)
  • Up to 5-year battery life per mote
  • Self-healing mesh topology
  • Encrypted data at the mote level
  • 50+ motes per gateway
🔌

Additional Sensor Types

Utterberry platform supports additional mote types that can be added to the iSAVE system as needed.

  • Current transformer (CT) clamps — energy metering
  • Pressure transducers — refrigerant circuits
  • Acoustic emissions — leak detection
  • CO₂ / air quality sensors
  • Door / panel open detection

🏦 Carbon Credit Verification Pipeline

  • Baseline energy model stored on-chain at installation

  • Grid carbon intensity data timestamped and stored

Demand Response

Demand Response System Specification

How iSAVE switches assets on and off — safely, verifiably, and automatically

📡

DR Signal Sources

  • OpenADR 2.0: Utility VTN issues event → iSAVE VEN executes
  • Karnot Control Centre: Manual or AI-triggered dispatch
  • Price signal: Real-time tariff → auto-dispatch on threshold
  • Frequency response: Grid frequency deviation (under-frequency load shed)
  • Local: Solar surplus → auto charge thermal/electric battery
  • Manual override: Operator app override always available
🔄

Curtailment Sequence Logic

  • Step 1: Check comfort limits — is zone temp within acceptable range?
  • Step 2: Calculate available DR duration (thermal mass, battery SoC)
  • Step 3: Pre-condition if time allows (store thermal energy before event)
  • Step 4: Execute curtailment — heat pump off, battery discharge to maintain comfort
  • Step 5: Monitor comfort — abort and restart if limits breached
  • Step 6: Event ends — staggered restart (avoid rebound spike)
  • Step 7: Log all to Rubidex for verification
⏱️

Switching Duration Limits (Per Asset)

  • Heat pump (cooling mode): Up to 4 hours off (thermal mass dependent)
  • Heat pump (heating mode): Up to 2 hours off (thermal mass dependent)
  • Electric water heater: Up to 6 hours off
  • Thermal battery discharge: Up to 8 hours (SoC dependent)
  • Electric battery: Up to 4 hours (SoC and DoD limit)
  • Minimum off-time between events: 30 minutes (compressor protection)
  • All limits configurable per site by Karnot engineer
🛡️

Safety & Comfort Overrides

  • Hard temperature limits: never let space exceed ±3°C comfort band
  • Compressor minimum-off timer (prevents short-cycling)
  • Maximum continuous-on limit (thermal stress protection)
  • Refrigerant pressure watchdog — emergency restart if out of range
  • Customer override: resident can always override via app
  • Medical priority sites: never curtail (configurable flag)
